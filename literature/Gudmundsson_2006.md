Flock defined in Benkert_2006, Laube_2002, Laube_2004, Gudmundsson  and  Kreveled's definition:

$flock(m, k, r)$

Flock: a flock in a time interval is at least m individuals with every point within time  interval of duration k are within minimum [distance](distance.md) r 
- fixed flock: all individuals stay together during the entire interval
- varying flock: individuals may change but minimum m individuals present
Meet: similar to flock but considers a stationary, fixed location 
- fixed meet: all individuals stay together during the entire interval
- varying meet: individuals may change but minimum m individuals present
