Lagrangian approach: individuals are observed and followed along their movement patterns over time

> while purely geometric algorithms advanced our ability to find structures and patterns in movement data sets, the extracted patterns were lacking semantics and a clear connection to the processes creating these patterns. Incorporation of context information and domain knowledge is essential to the validation and interpretation of patterns. Having meaningful research questions and hypotheses about the underlying processes captured in the data can increase the validity and applicability of developed computational methods.
