Li, Ming Fei, T. Jean M. Arseneau-Robar, Eve A. Smeltzer, and Julie A. Teichroeb. 2021. “Be Early or Be Tolerated: Vervet Monkey, Chlorocebus Pygerythrus, Foraging Strategies in a Dispersed Resource.” _Animal Behaviour_ 176 (June): 1–15. [https://doi.org/10.1016/j.anbehav.2021.03.010](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.anbehav.2021.03.010).

Beyond binary [producer](producer.md) [scrounger](scrounger.md) strategies, looked at different kinds of scrounging: displacement scrounging, tolerated scrounging, untolerated scrounging

Mention that [[GPS]] could help clarify how producers gain greater solitary foraging time advantages by clarifying the sequency of events leading up to a the discovery of a food patch
