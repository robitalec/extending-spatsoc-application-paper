Data

	- After filtering, left with 10-37.5 hours of data per group

Methods
- [[group centroid]] defined as the average position at each time point
- future velocity vector = vector pointing from position at time t to next position > 10 m away
- past velocity vector = vector pointing to position at time t from previous position > 10 m away
- turn influence and speed influence = X component and Y component (respectively) that measure the contribution of an individual's vector to the group's vector
- "**Positional turn influence** is defined as the probability that the group turns in a given direction (right or left) as a function of an individual’s position to the left or right of the group center. Similarly, **positional speed influence** is defined as the probability that the group speeds up as a function of the front-back position of an individual"
