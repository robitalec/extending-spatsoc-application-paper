Automated methods for assessing social [dominance](dominance.md), in-flight leader-follower

In both types of behaviour the authors found hierarchically structured networks of directed interactions. [Dominance](dominance.md) and leadership are not conflated, they are independent and the authors hypothesize that the leadership hierarchies must be based on a different  set of criteria than [dominance](dominance.md). 

Mini GPS and  computer vision based methods

Manually scored agonistic interactions to compare how human observations compare to automated [dominance](dominance.md) analysis

OpenCV

Methods for leader-follower follow [Nagy_2010](Nagy_2010.md)

Two automated [dominance](dominance.md) networks were generated:

Feeding-queuing

- feeding if within <20 cm from the cup, head pointing toward the center +- 30 degrees
- queuing if within <60 cm from the cup, not feeding
- for bird i, time queuing next to bird j divided by total time that i and j were either feeding or queuing

Approach-avoidance

- time-averaged dot product of i's velocity and direction from i to j
- averaged across all frame where i, j were within 50 cm of each other
- AA is positive if i tends to approach j anad negative if i tends to avoid j


Given the matrix of interactions, measure symmetry (0-1) where low represents a society where interactions are dominant in one direction only and measure transitivity (0.5-1) where higher values  correspond to fewer loops in the [hierarchy](hierarchy.md), loops indicating where the [hierarchy](hierarchy.md) is non-linear, vs transitive/linear

[Dominance](dominance.md) indices used for manually scored networks

- Kalinoski’s frequency of success index (Kalinoski 1975 Condor)
- Lindquist’s dominance index (Lindquist 2009 Bull Math Biol)
- Batchelder-Bershad-Simpson scale score (Jameson 1999 Anim Behav)
- de Vries’ (modified) (normalized) David’s scores (Bayly 2006 Behav Processes)

Since this example's network was highly transitive, these [[dominance]] indices were all very similar with a correlation between all four being >0.99.
